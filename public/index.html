About This Application

Overview

This Pipeline Valuation System provides access to FERC Form 6 data and enables comprehensive pipeline valuations using industry-standard approaches.

Features

  • FERC Database Search: Search through 250+ pipeline companies (2024 Q4 XBRL filings)
  • Tri-Approach Valuation: Cost, Income, and Market approaches
  • Company Details: Comprehensive XBRL data extraction with financial and operational metrics
  • Evaluation History: Track and compare valuations over time

Valuation Methodologies

Cost Approach

Estimates value based on replacement cost new less depreciation. Considers physical deterioration, functional obsolescence, and economic obsolescence.

Income Approach

Uses discounted cash flow (DCF) analysis to value the pipeline based on its ability to generate future income. Applies appropriate discount rates based on risk profile.

Market Approach

Values the pipeline based on comparable transactions and market multiples from similar pipeline assets. Adjusts for differences in size, location, and operational characteristics.

Data Source

Data is sourced from FERC Form 6 filings, which are annual reports filed by oil pipeline companies with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission.
